# 📖 GitScore User Guide

Welcome to the Sanctuary of Code. This guide will help you understand your coordinates and how to ascend the ranks of the GitScore hierarchy.

## ✧ Understanding Your Score

Your total score (0-100) is a weighted aggregation of 4 mystical dimensions:

1. **Technical Depth (35pts)**: Complexity of the scrolls you write. Measures lines of code, algorithmic variety, and repository health.
2. **Social Impact (25pts)**: Your influence in the code rift. Calculated via stars, forks, and followers.
3. **Activity Consistency (25pts)**: The frequency of your rituals. Measures your contribution streak and weekly commitment.
4. **Scrying Innovation (15pts)**: Use of cutting-edge technologies and participation in emerging constellations (trending repos).

## 🏆 Rank Hierarchy

- **Rank S (90+)**: Master of the Void. High impact, extreme consistency, and broad technical depth.
- **Rank A (80-89)**: Elite Explorer. Strong presence and technical proficiency.
- **Rank B (70-79)**: Skilled Artisan. Solid contributions and regular activity.
- **Rank C (60-69)**: Budding Scryer. Active but with room for technical expansion.
- **Rank F (<60)**: Apprentice. Just beginning their journey into the coordinates.

## 🚀 How to Improve Your Score

- **Document the Unknown**: Add descriptions and READMEs to your repositories.
- **Consistency is Key**: Avoid large bursts followed by silence. Regular, small commits show better consistency.
- **Join Great Rituals**: Contribute to popular open-source projects to boost your Social Impact.
- **Diversify Your Spells**: Use multiple languages and frameworks across your projects.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why is my score lower than expected?**
A: GitScore focuses on the *quality* and *consistency* of public activity. Private contributions are currently shielded from our scrying lens.

**Q: How often does my score update?**
A: Our edge caching refreshes every hour. Once the cooldown expires, the engine will re-analyze your profile upon the next visit.
